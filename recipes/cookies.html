Cookies

This is an image of a few cookies, the recipe below will instruct in how to make some for yourself. They may not look as good.

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • chocolate chips
  • flour
  • water

Preparation

    Mix the flour, water and sugar into a glass bowl until it looks senisble to you..
    Tear off pieces of the dough and form balls from them, then lay them flat on a baking tray covered with parchment paper.
    Add the chocolate chips.
    Place in the oven at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
