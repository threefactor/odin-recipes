Lasagna

This is an image of a block of lasagna; meat, cheese... the works. This recipe will demystify how to put it all together.

Ingredients

  • meat
  • cheese
  • pasta
  • tomato sauce

Preparation

    Place a layer of cooked lasagna pasta first inside some glassware
    Cover the paste with the tomato sauce
    Then place a layer of ground meat, then a layer of cheese.
    Repeat this process until you have a desired thickness for your meal...however i think you should stop after 3 or 4 layers.
    Place in the oven at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
